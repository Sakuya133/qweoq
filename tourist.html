Ubud

Ubud is a district in Bali that holds great value in terms of culture and tourism. It is well known for its art and culinary scene. In addition, a royal palace still stands next to Ubud Market, remaining preserved to this day and often attracting tourists.

Bali Zoo

Bali Zoo is a great place for relaxation when visiting Gianyar. Visitors can see a wide variety of animals from Indonesia and around the world. Accommodation is also available for those who wish to stay overnight.

Tirta Empul Temple

Tirta Empul Temple is a religious tourism destination ideal for finding peace of mind. It features a natural spring considered sacred, used by Balinese Hindus for purification rituals known as melukat. The temple is also a popular spot for visitors seeking a spiritual atmosphere and tranquility.

Stadium Captain I Wayan Dipta

Stadium Captain I Wayan Dipta is the largest football stadium in Bali, located in Gianyar. It serves as the home ground of Bali United and regularly hosts both national and international matches. With a capacity of tens of thousands of spectators, the stadium has become one of the key sports landmarks in Bali.

Lebih Beach

Lebih Beach is one of the well-known beaches in Gianyar, Bali. It features unique volcanic black sand and a natural, tranquil atmosphere. Visitors can not only enjoy the ocean view but also taste local delicacies made from fresh fish served in many food stalls along the beach.
